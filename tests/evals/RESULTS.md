| Arm | What | Input tokens | vs. A |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| A | no-caveman (baseline)        | 2555 | — |
| B | role only (prepended)        | 3085 | -20.7% |
| C | role + compressed bundle     | 3004 | -17.6% |

Arm B is **larger** than A by design — the role itself costs tokens. Its return on investment is on the **output** side (what the model writes), which this harness does not measure. Arm C compresses both the role and the bundle; whether C drops below A depends on how prose-heavy the bundle is. Terse bundles (tables, bullets) recover less of the role's cost than prose-heavy ones.
